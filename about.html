Virendra Textrioum began as an exclusive atelier entirely devoted to redefining Indian menswear. Our mission transcends clothing; it is the pursuit of capturing power, elegance, and heritage in every stitch.

We drape the discerning gentleman in majestic silhouettes that honor centuries of artisanal expertise, from meticulously tailored sherwanis to fluid khadi kurtas designed for a commanding presence.
